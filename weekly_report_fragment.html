Public art opportunities can be difficult to track because open calls, commissions, sketch assignments, consultant roles, and application documents are often spread across separate official websites. The Public Art Watcher brings selected opportunities into one readable page, organized by country, with deadlines, budgets, opportunity types, and direct links to the original source. It is designed for visual artists, public artists, and studio teams who want to scan serious opportunities faster, compare what is currently open, and decide which calls are worth deeper attention.

The Public Art Watcher is an independent research and monitoring tool. It is not an official application portal and does not replace the original source pages. Each listing should be treated as a starting point for your own review. Deadlines, budgets, eligibility rules, attachments, and application links can change after they are detected here. Before applying, always open the official source page and confirm the current requirements with the organizer, commissioning body, or public art agency.

The page currently begins with selected Nordic sources, and it will grow from there. I hope it helps you save time, notice stronger opportunities earlier, and put more energy into the applications that truly fit your work.
